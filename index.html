Residential Plans With Clear Next Steps

Homeowners and contractors work with Timpson Drafting and Design for clear drawings, practical project planning, and residential design services that fit the scope of each project. Remote drafting is available across the United States, while on-site field work is concentrated around Colorado City, Northern Arizona, and Southern Utah.

Service Area and Project Location

Timpson can support residential drafting projects nationwide when clients can share photos, measurements, sketches, and permit details. Local field-measurement work is primarily suited to Colorado City, Northern Arizona, and Southern Utah projects.

Garage and Addition Plans

Timpson prepares residential drafting plans for detached garages, ADUs, home additions, bedroom suites, mudrooms, and extra living space. Plan sets are scoped around the existing home, site conditions, and the drawings needed for homeowner, contractor, and permitting review.

Remodel and Renovation Drawings

Remodel and renovation work often starts with documenting existing conditions before new design drawings can be completed. Timpson supports kitchen, bath, whole-home renovation, and residential remodel planning with clear drafting documents.

Permit-Ready Construction Documents

Permit-ready construction documents are organized drawing packages prepared for review by the appropriate building department. Requirements vary by project and jurisdiction, so the drafting package is reviewed against the specific project scope.

As-Built Drawings

As-built drawings document existing structures for remodel planning, additions, repair work, contractor coordination, and project records. Accurate existing-condition drawings help clarify what is already built before new work is planned.

Custom Home Design Support

Timpson works with homeowners and contractors on custom residential design support, construction drawing updates, and project-specific drafting packages for new homes and residential projects.
