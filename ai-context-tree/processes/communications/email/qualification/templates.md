# Email Qualification Templates

## Node Type

Template reference.

## Purpose

These are lightweight template patterns for the email qualification workflow. They are not final brand copy; they are routing-safe starting points.

## Template: Missing Fields

Subject pattern:

`A few details needed for your quote request`

Body pattern:

`Thanks for reaching out. To help us review your project, please reply with the missing details about your project type, location, scope, and timeline. Once we have that, we can follow up with next steps.`

## Template: Invalid Email

Operational note:

This branch usually cannot rely on email delivery. Use it mainly as a classification state and route toward alternate contact handling where permitted.

## Template: No Response

Subject pattern:

`Following up on your drafting inquiry`

Body pattern:

`Checking back on your request. If you still want help with your project, reply here with any updates and we can continue from there.`

## Template: Qualified

Subject pattern:

`We received your project inquiry`

Body pattern:

`Thanks for contacting Timpson Drafting & Design. We reviewed your request and will follow up with next steps for your project shortly.`

## Template: Home Addition Missing Scope

Subject pattern:

`A few details about your addition project`

Body pattern:

`Thanks for reaching out about your addition project. Please reply with the type of addition, approximate size, project location, whether you have any plans or photos, and any important details about how it connects to the existing house. Once we have that, we can review next steps.`

## Template: Text Fallback

Operational note:

Do not send a text from this file. This template exists only to explain the handoff. The actual channel switch should be handled by the text subtree.
