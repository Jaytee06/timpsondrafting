# Email Qualification Templates

## Node Type

Template reference.

## Purpose

These are lightweight template patterns for the email qualification workflow. They are not final brand copy; they are routing-safe starting points.

Every outbound message derived from these templates should still be reviewed for professionalism before sending.

## Template: Missing Fields

Subject pattern:

`A few details needed for your quote request`

Body pattern:

`Thanks for reaching out. To help us review your project, please reply with the missing details about your project type, location, scope, and timeline. Once we have that, we can follow up with next steps.`

Operational note:

Use this only when a broad missing-information request is still appropriate. If the lead already replied with substantive project details, send a narrower custom clarification message instead of repeating a generic catch-all request.

## Template: Contradictory Reply Clarification

Subject pattern:

`One quick clarification about your project`

Body pattern:

`Thanks for the details. I want to confirm one point before we move forward. Your latest reply appears to conflict with the earlier project information we have on file. Please confirm which project type is correct, and reply with any one or two remaining details still needed to continue.`

## Template: Invalid Email

Operational note:

This branch usually cannot rely on email delivery. Use it mainly as a classification state and route toward alternate contact handling where permitted.

## Template: No Response

Subject pattern:

`Following up on your drafting inquiry`

Body pattern:

`Checking back on your request. If you still want help with your project, reply here with any updates and we can continue from there.`

Operational note:

Use this only when no newer substantive reply exists. Do not use it after a lead has already replied with meaningful project details.

## Template: Qualified

Subject pattern:

`We received your project inquiry`

Body pattern:

`Thanks for contacting Timpson Drafting & Design. We reviewed your request and will follow up with next steps for your project shortly.`

## Template: Home Addition Missing Scope

Subject pattern:

`A few details about your addition project`

Body pattern:

`Thanks for reaching out about your addition project. Please reply with the type of addition, approximate size, project location, whether you have any plans or photos, and any important details about how it connects to the existing house. Once we have that, we can review next steps.`

Operational note:

If the lead already supplied most of these details, replace this with a narrower custom clarification that asks only for the remaining unknowns.

## Template: Text Fallback

Operational note:

Do not send a text from this file. This template exists only to explain the handoff. The actual channel switch should be handled by the text subtree.

## Professional Standard

No template in this file authorizes sending:

- placeholder text
- junk or debugging text
- partial drafts
- stale content copied from another lead

If the composer shows any of those conditions, cancel the send and treat the attempt as blocked.
