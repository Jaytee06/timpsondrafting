# Branch: Text Fallback

## Node Type

Branch outcome.

## Meaning

Email is not the best current communication path, and SMS or phone-based follow-up is the next operational move.

## Preconditions

- phone contact exists,
- text consent is present or otherwise operationally valid,
- email is absent, invalid, or not producing progress.

## Local Objective

Transfer responsibility from the email branch to the text communication branch with enough context to avoid re-qualifying from scratch.

## Required Handoff Context

- lead identity,
- phone number,
- consent state,
- summary of attempted email handling,
- current qualification judgment,
- missing information still needed.

## Escalation Rules

- consent language is ambiguous,
- phone data is present but likely invalid,
- local text handling rules are missing or conflict with policy.
