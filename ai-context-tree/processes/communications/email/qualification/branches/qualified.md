# Branch: Qualified

## Node Type

Branch outcome.

## Meaning

The lead is in scope, contactable, and sufficiently detailed for normal follow-up.

## Typical Signals

- real person or business identifier,
- usable email or alternate contact path,
- clear project type or understandable project description,
- enough scope detail to continue without guessing.

## Local Objective

Move the lead forward with normal response handling instead of additional clarification routing.

## Allowed Local Decisions

- classify as `qualified`,
- prepare or send the standard acknowledgment,
- move into the next downstream sales or quote process,
- preserve attribution context for reporting.

## Escalation Rules

- qualification criteria are disputed,
- the project is borderline out of scope,
- the downstream CRM workflow requires different status mapping.
