Local permitting considerations

Building requirements vary by city, county, property, occupancy, and scope. Identify the authority having jurisdiction and request its current submittal checklist. Timpson will coordinate the agreed drafting scope, while the authority decides whether engineering, architecture, energy documents, or other supporting work is required.
