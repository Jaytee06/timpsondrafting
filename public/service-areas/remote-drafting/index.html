Nationwide Remote Residential Drafting

Timpson coordinates suitable residential drafting projects throughout the United States when clients and project teams can provide reliable property information, measurements, photographs, surveys, and jurisdiction requirements.

Request a remote project quoteCall (435) 319-5331

How remote drafting works

  1. Send the project location, goals, files, and schedule.
  2. Timpson reviews whether the source information is sufficient.
  3. Missing measurements or jurisdiction questions are resolved before production.
  4. Drawings and revisions are coordinated electronically.

When local help may be needed

Additions, remodels, and as-built work can require field verification. Structural, site, or jurisdiction requirements may also require a local architect, engineer, surveyor, or other professional.
