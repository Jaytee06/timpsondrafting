What Timpson is focused on

Timpson is positioned as a residential drafting and design business, not a general-everything architecture site. The clearest fit is custom residential work involving homes, garages, additions, remodels, as-built drawings, and permit-ready document coordination.

The office address shown on the site is 10 Central St Suite 205, Colorado City, AZ 86021, and the current public contact methods are phone and email.

Common projects clients ask about

Custom residential plans

Homes and smaller custom layouts that need practical room flow, clear dimensions, and organized drafting before contractor review and permit prep.

Garages, ADUs, and additions

Projects that need to fit an existing home, lot, and permit path before the drawings can be scoped confidently.

Remodel and as-built work

Existing-condition documentation and renovation drawing support when a project needs to understand what is already there before moving forward.

Permit-ready document coordination

Residential drawing packages organized around the project scope and local review process instead of one fixed template for every job.

Why people reach out early

  • They want to know what information helps define a drafting quote.

  • They want a practical next step before talking to contractors or preparing for permit review.
