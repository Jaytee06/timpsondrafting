How Much Do Drafting Services Cost?

The scope, existing information, revisions, site, and jurisdiction factors that affect drafting fees.

By Timpson Drafting & Design · Published July 20, 2026 · Updated July 20, 2026

Drafting cost follows scope and uncertainty

There is no responsible single price for every home project. A small revision to an editable plan is fundamentally different from measuring an existing home, designing an addition, or coordinating a new custom-home package.

Use these pricing questions

  • Are reliable source drawings available and editable?
  • How much existing-condition measurement is required?
  • Which sheets and revisions are included?
  • Are site, structural, energy, or other consultants separate?
  • Who coordinates permit comments after the first submission?

Timpson provides a project-based quote after reviewing the location, approximate size, intended work, source files, and schedule. The clearest way to control cost is to resolve missing information and decision ownership before production begins.
